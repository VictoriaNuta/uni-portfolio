Learning Journey

The Learning Journey

Over 11 weeks, I covered a range of topics in the computer science course, starting with data and databases in Week 1. I learned about database modelling, SQL, web programming, cloud computing, content management systems, AI, game development, data science, HCI, and UX in subsequent weeks. In Week 11, I reviewed and consolidated my learning, and helped me to complete the final assignment. This learning journey equipped me with valuable skills and knowledge in computer science.

WEEK 2 – Database Modelling
WEEK 3 – Relational Database and SQL
WEEK 4 – Web Programming
WEEK 5 – Cloud Computing
WEEK 6 – Content Management Systems and WordPress
WEEK 7 – Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)
WEEK 8 – Game Development
WEEK 9 – Data Science
WEEK 10 – Human Computer Interaction (HCI) and User Experience (UX)
WEEK 11 – Revision and Assignment Workshop
